Fu-Yun Wang
fywang@link.cuhk.edu.hk

Fu-Yun Wang (Pronounced as "Foo-Yoon Wahng" IPA: [fu˧˥ yn˧˥ wɑŋ]) is a second-year Ph.D. student at MMLab@CUHK, supervised by Professor Xiaogang Wang and Professor Hongsheng Li.

Prior to this, he obtained his B.Eng. from Nanjing University in 2023 with first-class honor, supervised by Professor Han-Jia Ye and Dr. Da-Wei Zhou in the LAMDA Group.

Currently, he works on diffusion acceleration. He is also interested in building innovative generative vision applications. He works closely with Dr. Zhaoyang Huang.
